Poll monitor instructions & information

Poll assignment instructions

You will be assigned an area - Southwest, Central City, Downtown, North, or Northwest.
Choose any polling location in that area. See the list below.
Click on the addresses in the list to open Google Maps directions.
Try to go to each of the polling locations in your area at least once.
Use your judgement to decide when to move on to other polling locations in your assigned area.
More than one poll monitor may be assigned to the same area. If you run into another poll monitor, you can team up if you wish, or divide up work. It's up to you.
